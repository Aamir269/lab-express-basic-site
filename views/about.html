About Imran Khan

Imran Khan is a former cricketer and the current Prime Minister of Pakistan. He founded the political party Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) and became Prime Minister in 2018. His tenure has been marked by efforts to combat corruption and implement social welfare programs, but it has also faced criticism and challenges.

Imran Khan's key achievements include founding the Shaukat Khanum Cancer Hospital, establishing Namal University, and leading Pakistan to victory in the 1992 Cricket World Cup. He is also known for his philanthropic work and became Prime Minister of Pakistan in 2018.
